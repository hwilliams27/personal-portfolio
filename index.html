Hello There!

I am a junior in the Macaulay Honors Program at John Jay who is majoring in Computer Science and Information Security and minoring in Digital Media and Journalism.

As a current CSCI student at John Jay, I joined the TTP Residency to gain more hands-on experience as a developer. While there, I learned full stack web development utilizing both front-end and back-end technologies, such as Javascript, Node.js, Bootstrap, React.js, and Python. We also had the opportunity to work in groups to build websites utilizing Git and Github and services like Trello, Discord and Slack for project management and communicating. The potential to innovate, connect, and create with technology has always piqued my interest. My current aspiration is to be a software engineer, but I am still open to exploring the other endless opportunities that come within the tech space such as cybersecurity, data science, and ux design. Currently, I am working on a personal website where I can display projects I’m working on and art that I make to better understand my strengths and weaknesses in web development and improve my skills. I have always wanted to be a part of changing and growing our abilities as a society for the future, and the tech industry is the prime space to reach that goal. From seeing the lack of women in the tech space since a young age, I aspire to serve as a role model for younger girls to show that the tech field is an inviting space for them. During my time finishing my undergrad, I want to continue to explore the many facets within the tech space and find where I fit.
